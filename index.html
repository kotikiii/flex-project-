Properties for flex-container

display: flex;

the property of flex container to use flexbox

flex-diraction: row;

how flex items are placed in the flex container defining the main axis and the direction

flex-wrap: nowrap

pushing elements to second line in case of shortage of space

flex-flew: row nowrap

short form for properties "flex-direction" and "flex-wrap"

justify-content: flex-start;

defines how the browser distributes space between and around content items along the main-axis of a flex container

align-content: stretch;

property sets the distribution of space between and around content items along a flexbox's cross-axis
